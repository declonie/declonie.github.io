GET Longboards: Black Knight

Black Knight was created for a commercial longboard company in Grand Rapids. GET Longboards asked our advertising class to come up with new designs for several of their boards and this is one of the many that I created. Inspired by old RPG's such as Final Fantasy, the Black Knight is a board that shows off the sheer power that old school knight characters had in classic RPGs.
