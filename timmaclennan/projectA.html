GET Longboards: Cali Cruiser

Cali Cruiser was created for a commercial longboard company in Grand Rapids. GET Longboards asked our advertising class to come up with new designs for several of their boards and this is one of the many that I created. Inspired by carnival piers on the west coast, Cali Cruiser attempts to create a colorful experience that reminds skaters about light-hearted carnival fun.
