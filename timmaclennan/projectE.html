Student Planner

The Student Handbook & Planner was created to help the Advertising Department at Michigan State University. The faculty wanted a way for students to always have a copy of the student handbook with them at all times.

The Challenge: College students are constantly on the go, and many different things are often fighting for a spot in a typical students bag. They don't have room for things that they deem less important or something situational that they will not use everyday.

My Solution: By combining all of the rules and messages of the Advertising Handbook with the convenience and utility of a daily planner, I was able to create a small, easy to use, and functional planner that students could carry with them at all times.
