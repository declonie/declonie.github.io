GET Longboards: Anticz

Antics was created for a commercial longboard company in Grand Rapids. GET Longboards asked our advertising class to come up with new designs for several of their boards and this is one of the many that I created. Inspired by classic cartoon antics, tomfoolery, and shenanigans, this board pays tribute to one of the best moments that would happen in an old cartoon.
