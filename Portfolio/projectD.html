Personal Branding

Personal Branding - Design Adventures was created to comically detail my life as I work on designs. I, the purple jelly creature, just recently graduated from college with a large amount of student debt, which forces me to join the working class world and find a job. This project was set up to look and sound like an old-school video game manual that contains all of the charm of the classics from the 80's and 90's.
