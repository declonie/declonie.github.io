BadAd

BadAd is a prototype board game that was designed to teach kids the dangers of unhealthy food advertising.

Rules of the Game: Every player starts in the dotted box labeled "Start." Before each turn, players draw a card, if they answer the question on the card correctly, they then use the spinner located on the right side of the board to determine which color that they land on. The player then moves their token to the closest square of that color. If a player answers the question incorrectly, they must skip their turn. The goal of the game is to reach the end of the board.

Checkpoint System: When a player passes by a checkpoint system they will be asked a question, if the player gets the question correct, they will get to traverse on the shorter, "Healthy" path, if the player gets the question wrong, they will have to travel on the longer, "Unhealthy" path.
